How long does implementation take?

Most schools are up and running within 2-4 weeks. We provide dedicated onboarding specialists who will guide you through data migration, staff training, and configuration based on your needs.

Yes. We use enterprise-grade encryption, role-based access, regular audits, and comply with FERPA and GDPR.
